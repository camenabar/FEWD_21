.05 Press Uncuffed Branding

  • Date Spring 2015
  • Description Press Uncuffed is a campaign that supports the fight to free imprisoned journalists across the globe. When you buy a bracelet, proceeds will go to the Committee to Protect Journalists. The bracelet serves as a reminder of the courage and struggle of journalists out there who are wearing a cuff of their own.
